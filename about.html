Pine City Mall is a double-story mall located in the heart of Pinetown, in KwaZulu-Natal. This giant hosts a wide variety of shops which consumer go and spend there hard earned money. Some of the popular shops include Game, Checkers, CNA , Pep, Pick N Pay, Mr Price and many more. Pine City Mall does not have a lot of restaurants but Steers is probably the most popular of all the restaurants at the mall. However there a quite a few fast foods outlets lets list a few Debonairs which sell Pizza, Nandos and Kfc do chicken and Wimpy does either sit or takeaway burgers. Pine City Mall has all the major South African bank in it premises and there is a place to exchange your money to Rand most major currency's are excepted. On the Entertainment side there is not a lot to do. However there is Movie theatre upstairs and a Virgin Active Gym for you to go to while your wife goes shopping. Pine City Mall is a fully enclosed mall with quite a lot of parking both under cover and with no cover.
