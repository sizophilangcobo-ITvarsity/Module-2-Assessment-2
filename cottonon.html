COTTON ON

Cotton On Group is Australia's largest global retailer, known for its fashion clothing and stationery brands. It has over 1,500 stores in 18 countries and employs 22,000 workers globally. It currently operates eight brands: Cotton On, Cotton On Kids, the sub-brand ‘Free’, Cotton On Body, Factorie, Typo, Rubi, Supré, and Cotton On Foundation. Although strongly focused on clothing, the company also commercialises Homeware and stationery products through the Typo brand. Cotton On is also main sponsor of the AFL Women's, the Australian rules football league for female players.
